About LG Signature Suites

A GREAT URBAN HOTEL

Luxury Redefined, Affordable by Design

Welcome to LG Signature Suites, a premier 3-star hotel located in the heart of Kalyan Nagar, Bangalore. Nestled in one of the city's most vibrant neighborhoods, our hotel offers a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and affordability. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, LG Signature Suites provides a luxurious yet budget-friendly stay, ensuring that every guest experiences the best of Bangalore hospitality.

A Gateway to Kalyan Nagar

Situated in the upscale locality of Kalyan Nagar, LG Signature Suites is ideally positioned to offer easy access to Bangalore's top attractions. From bustling shopping centers and fine-dining restaurants to corporate hubs and cultural landmarks, everything you need is just moments away. Our central location makes us the perfect choice for travelers seeking both work and play.

Affordable Luxury

At LG Signature Suites, we believe that luxury should be accessible to everyone. As a 3-star hotel, we combine elegant accommodations, modern amenities, and exceptional service—all at surprisingly affordable rates. Our thoughtfully designed suites provide a haven of comfort, featuring plush furnishings, contemporary decor, and state-of-the-art facilities to ensure a restful and rejuvenating stay.

Dining Delights

Food is an integral part of your stay at LG Signature Suites. Our hotel boasts a restaurant and café, where guests can indulge in a wide array of culinary delights. Whether you're craving authentic Indian flavors, international cuisines, or freshly brewed coffee and light bites, our talented chefs are committed to delivering delicious meals made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Enjoy a hearty breakfast to start your day, unwind with a leisurely lunch, or savor a romantic dinner in a warm and inviting ambiance.

Nearby Attractions

Explore Bangalore's Best

UB City

Bangalore's premier luxury shopping destination, UB City offers high-end brands, fine dining restaurants, and entertainment options. A perfect place for shopping and dining experiences.

~9 km from Kalyan Nagar

Commercial Street

One of Bangalore's most popular shopping destinations, Commercial Street offers everything from traditional Indian wear to modern fashion, accessories, and street food. A shopper's paradise.

~10 km from Kalyan Nagar

Nandi Hills

A popular weekend getaway, Nandi Hills offers breathtaking sunrise views, trekking trails, and a peaceful retreat from the city. Perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

~60 km from Kalyan Nagar

Cubbon Park

One of Bangalore's most iconic landmarks, Cubbon Park is a 300-acre green lung in the heart of the city. Perfect for morning walks, jogging, and picnics amidst lush greenery and historic monuments.

~8 km from Kalyan Nagar

Bangalore Palace

A magnificent palace built in Tudor-style architecture, Bangalore Palace is a must-visit for history enthusiasts. Explore the royal heritage and beautiful gardens surrounding this architectural marvel.

~10 km from Kalyan Nagar

Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Home to rare species of flora and the famous glass house, Lalbagh is a paradise for nature lovers. Enjoy the serene environment, flower shows, and beautiful walking trails.

~12 km from Kalyan Nagar

ISKCON Temple

One of the largest ISKCON temples in the world, this spiritual destination offers a serene atmosphere for meditation and worship. The beautiful architecture and peaceful ambiance make it a popular attraction.

~6 km from Kalyan Nagar

Bannerghatta National Park

Experience wildlife up close at this national park featuring a zoo, safari, butterfly park, and rescue center. Perfect for families and wildlife enthusiasts looking for an adventurous day out.

~25 km from Kalyan Nagar

Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace

A historical monument showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture, this palace was the summer residence of Tipu Sultan. Explore the rich history and beautiful teakwood architecture.

~15 km from Kalyan Nagar
